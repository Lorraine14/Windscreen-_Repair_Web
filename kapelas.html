FAQs

What services does Kapela Autoglass offer?

We specialize in windscreen and window replacement as well as chip repairs for all vehicle types.

How do I know if my windshield needs repair or replacement?

Minor chips may be repaired, but cracks in the driver’s view often require full replacement.

Do you offer mobile services?

Yes. We come to your home, office, or wherever you are to repair or replace your auto glass.

How long does it take to replace a windshield?

Most replacements take 1–2 hours depending on your vehicle type and complexity of the installation.

Can I drive immediately after a replacement?

It’s recommended to wait at least one hour after installation to ensure adhesives have properly cured.

What should I do if I have a chip or crack?

Have it looked at immediately to avoid the damage spreading. Small chips can often be fixed quickly.

Is Kapela's open on weekends?

Yes, we offer flexible hours including weekends. Contact us to set up a convenient appointment.
